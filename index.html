    About the Fortuitous Critic

    The Fortuitous Critic travels the world for business and pleasure, staying at some of the premier hotels and resorts around the globe. Most importantly, however, the Fortuitous Critic has very high standards.

    TripAdvisor, Hotels.com, and other websites with hotel reviews often do not live up to the Fortuitous Critic's standards. The Fortuitous Critic's reviews are designed for the discerning traveler who seeks the highest quality in world travel.

    The Fortuitous Critic reviews primarily 4 and 5-star hotels and airport lounges on a unique scale inspired by golf handicaps. Read more about the rating system

    Rating System

    The Fortuitous Critic utilizes an unique rating scale inspired by golf handicaps. Every hotel and airport lounge reviewed includes a series of categories and features, which are rated relative to the Fortuitous Critic's expectations ("par"). Considering the Fortuitous Critic's high standards, it is very difficult to do better than par "birdie." In many cases, however, doing a lot worse than par is much below standard than just below par ("bogey" versus "triple bogey").
